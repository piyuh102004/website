Varkala Vibes - Cliffs & Coastal Bliss

Discover the perfect blend of nature, culture, and relaxation with our 7N/8D "Varkala Vibes - Cliffs & Coastal Bliss" package. Begin your journey with 2 nights in Munnar, where the cool climate and stunning tea plantations await. Next, explore the wildlife of Thekkady for 2 nights, before heading to Alleppey for a serene night aboard a traditional houseboat. Finally, unwind for 2 nights in the coastal paradise of Varkala, known for its breathtaking cliffs and golden beaches. This all-inclusive package covers round-trip flights, intercity car transfers, airport transfers, and stays at carefully selected 4-star, 3-star hotels, and a houseboat. Enjoy 7 exciting activities, including a Spice Plantation Tour, all curated by our expert tour manager, and savor traditional Kerala cuisine during your selected meals. Book this ultimate escape for an unforgettable journey through Kerala’s finest destinations!
